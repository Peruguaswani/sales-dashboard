# sales-dashboard
📊 Retail Sales Performance Dashboard – Business Analysis Project
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify key business insights and growth opportunities.
The objective was not only to build visualizations but to provide business-focused recommendations.

🎯 Business Questions Addressed

Which products generate the most revenue?

How do sales change over time?

Which segments and regions contribute the most?

Where should the business focus to grow faster?

🛠 Tools Used

Power BI

Excel (Data cleaning & preparation)

📊 Key KPIs

Total Sales: 129.36K

Total Profit: 5.34K

Profit Margin: 4.13%

Total Orders: 231

📈 Key Insights
1️⃣ Revenue Trend

Sales show seasonal variation, with peak performance during specific months.
Marketing efforts should be aligned with high-demand periods.

2️⃣ Top Products

A small group of products contributes significantly to total revenue.
Business should focus on promoting and maintaining inventory of these products.

3️⃣ Segment Performance

Consumer segment drives majority of revenue (~54%).
Corporate and Home Office segments offer expansion opportunities.

4️⃣ Regional Performance

Certain regions outperform others.
Strategic expansion and targeted campaigns can improve underperforming regions.

💡 Business Recommendations

Improve profit margins by focusing on high-margin products

Implement seasonal marketing strategies

Diversify product revenue sources

Expand in high-performing regions

Optimize pricing and supplier negotiations

📎 Deliverables

Interactive Power BI Dashboard

Business Insights Report

Actionable Growth Recommendations

🚀 Project Outcome

This project demonstrates practical data analytics skills including KPI analysis, trend evaluation, and business storytelling using data.
